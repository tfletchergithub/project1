Grilled Fish

Ingredients

  • 2 fish fillets (such as salmon or tilapia)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh herbs (such as dill or parsley)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.

2. Brush fish fillets with olive oil and season with salt and black pepper.

3. Place lemon slices on the fish fillets.

4. Grill the fish for about 4-5 minutes per side or until cooked through.

5. Sprinkle minced garlic and fresh herbs over the grilled fish.

6. Serve hot with your favorite side dishes.
