Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt and black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Saut√© pancetta and garlic until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and add grated cheeses.
  4. Drain cooked spaghetti and mix with egg and cheese mixture.
  5. Add pancetta, season with black pepper, and serve immediately.
