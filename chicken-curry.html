Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili, sauté for 2 minutes.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they become soft.
  4. Add curry powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until they are browned.
  6. Pour in coconut milk and simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with rice or naan.
